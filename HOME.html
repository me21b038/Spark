SPARK News Updates by Shaastra 2023

Shaastra, IIT Madras presents Spark, an exciting nation wide quiz specifically for students from classes 8-12. Do be there to feed your knowledge hungry brains and test your mettle against the best. Shaastra spark will be held in the following cities: Chennai, Pune, Kolkata, Delhi, Madurai, Cochin, Trivandrum, Calicut, Coimbatore, Bhilai, Trichi, Chandigarh and Mumbai.Shaastra is back with another edition of SPARK."Last year's edition of the Spark was conducted in 20 cities and saw a participation of more than 3000+ students across the nation."-says the Director of IITM.
