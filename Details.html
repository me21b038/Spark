SHAASTRA 2023

SPARK

Details

Structure:

Preliminary Round

  • An online quiz to test the logical reasoning and lateral thinking skills of students.
  • Students from classes 8 to 12 can participate in teams of 2.
  • A total of 70 teams across India will qualify for the screening round.

    • Screening Round

  • A problem statement will be provided to participants. Teams must come up with an innovative solution for the same.
  • The top 20 shortlisted teams will be invited to participate in the Spark Nationals at Shaastra, IIT Madras.

    • Spark Conference (Nationals)

  • Selected teams will be invited to IIT Madras on a sponsored two-day trip.
  • Finalists would participate in competitions, lectures, and guided campus tours to ensure a holistic learning experience.
  • Further details regarding the competition will be provided later to the selected teams.
