About


Shaastra strongly believes in knowledge sharing and encouraging scientific temperament among students across the country. In pursuit of turning our beliefs into reality, Shaastra has been conducting quizzes and conferences for school students under the banner of Spark (formerly SJQ) for over 10 years. Shaastra brings to you, the largest national level quiz competition by Shaastra which carries a great legacy dated to a decade, the first-ever online Spark for all school students to quiz at the comfort of their homes. Spark is a national platform where students compete with each other across technical quizzes, case studies, and presentations, and in the process improve themselves - all at no charge. Last year’s edition of the Spark Quiz was conducted in 20 cities and saw the participation of more than 3000+ students across the nation. From introducing quizzing to students to organizing a coveted nationwide inter-school competition, Spark has been growing in multifold since inception. With the exponential growth of participation and standard, Shaastra is back with yet another edition of Spark, the first round of which is going to be conducted in ‘Shaastra 2023’.
